Introduction

"Extra-Terrestrial Encroachers", is a game developed for the 2023 Junior Cert, using MakeCode Arcade. Inspired by classic arcade games like Space Invaders and Galaga, it offers an exciting space shooting experience.

Gameplay

In "Extra-Terrestrial Encroachers," players take control of a state-of-the-art spaceship to defend Earth from waves of invading aliens.
The objective is to shoot down the aliens while skillfully maneuvering the spaceship to avoid their relentless attacks.
The game offers a thrilling experience with multiple levels of increasing difficulty, each presenting new challenges and enemy types.
The game also features a captivating story mode where players embark on a chase after the aliens who kidnapped their beloved pet duck. It adds an extra layer of excitement and motivation to the gameplay experience.

Features

- Inspired by classic arcade games Space Invaders and Galaga

- Developed using MakeCode Arcade

- Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
